Research

My research focuses on broad areas of computational science: methods for solving Bayesian inverse problems such as data assimilation, theory-guided machine learning with both big and small data, and data-driven methods for both modeling and quantifying uncertainty in dynamical systems, among others. While a lot of the research that I do is classified as basic science, my broad goal is developing methods that are motivated by practical problems; my ideas should be no more than one step away from an application.
